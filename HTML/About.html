ENVISAGE





Envisage is a group of dedicated Senior Three ICT Students committed to reducing the environmental impact of electronic waste. We are located in Ndejje Senior Secondary School Uganda and we provide information on responsible e-waste disposal, recycling options, and the latest trends in sustainable technology. Our goal is to empower individuals and organisations to make informed choices and contribute to a greener future.
